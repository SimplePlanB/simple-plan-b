About Us

Pop-punk energy, tight set, and a crowd-first vibe. An all-queer band delivering full-throttle energy, real crowd interaction, and a guaranteed good time.

Ideal For

Performance Length

Simple Plan B offers high-energy sets ranging from 1–3 hours depending on the event and venue.

Pricing

Pricing is discussed upon booking request and may vary based on event details.

Additional Considerations

  • Travel outside the local Wilmington area

  • Special production or stage requirements
  • PA / sound system needs (if not provided by venue)

Promoter-Friendly Notes

Rates may adjust based on travel distance, set length, load-in time, and whether PA/sound is provided. If you have a budget, send it — we'll do our best to make it work.
