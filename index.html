Meet The Band

Meet Kate (she/her)

Lead Singer • Rhythm Guitarist

About Me

Kate is a lifelong competitive athlete turned self-taught musician — starting as a vocalist, then teaching herself guitar so she could write her own music and play live acoustic shows. She’s also a former law student, full-time spin instructor, and personal trainer.

Originally from Ohio, she moved to Raleigh for law school but discovered her true passion for spin while studying abroad in London. That passion stuck, and after meeting her wife in Raleigh — they’ve been married a year — she relocated to Wilmington to work for Bevin Prince at Recess.

Kate has always gravitated toward alternative bands like Mayday Parade, The Faim, and Halestorm. As a songwriter, she’s most inspired by powerhouse lyricists and vocalists — especially women — like Taylor Swift, Halsey, Fletcher, Lizzy McAlpine, Leanna Firestone, Maddie Zahm, and Gracie Abrams.

Meet Courtney (she/her)

Lead Guitar • Backup Vocals

About Me

Elder millennial, certified lesbian, ADHD blueprint, attention whore, wife of almost 20 years, and mom to the coolest 9-year-old kid.

Originally from Ohio, Courtney traded the Midwest for beach life in Wilmington, NC. By day, she works in aerospace. By night, she’s making art, internet chaos, and loud guitar noises.

Courtney picked up a guitar at 14, taught herself, and never really put it down. She spent her early 20s headbanging in heavy metal bands before “life” happened and now she’s back.

Raised on 80s hair metal, fully shaped by the pop-punk/emo/screamo pipeline, Courtney will listen to anything that makes her want to mosh like it’s Warped Tour 2004. If the beat slaps, she will rage.

Meet Reid (he/him)

Drummer

About Me

Reid is a percussionist, average golfer, and coffee connoisseur who was raised on Fleetwood Mac and Carolina beach music.

Originally from Central Virginia, he has lived in Fairfax, Virginia, West Palm Beach, Florida, and finally made his way to Wilmington in 2023 with his fiancée.

Reid is a trained musician who started piano at age six, taught himself guitar at ten, and also plays mandolin and bass. During the 2010s, he performed as the lead singer in a 90s cover band.

He grew up listening to country, 70s rock, and everything in between. Some of his favorite bands include Mayday Parade, Rise Against, Fleetwood Mac, and The Aces. His passion for drumming came from listening to Mick Fleetwood as a child.

When he's not behind the drum kit, Reid enjoys spending time on the water, kayaking, camping with his fiancée and pets, and traveling.

Meet Kat (she/her)

Bassist

About Me

Kat is a Nintendo nerd and a lifelong softball junkie who was raised on mosh pits and hanging out with the scene kids. If it’s loud, fast, or broken, she’s probably into it.

Originally from New York, Kat moved to Wilmington to start a new chapter by the sea with her wife.

Kat is a self-taught musician who plays everything by ear. She started on piano at 12, grabbed a guitar at 16, and has dabbled in just about any instrument she can get her hands on.

Kat grew up in the MySpace music era, fueled by emo and post-hardcore. Some of her favorite bands include Knocked Loose, Counterparts, Balance & Composure, and Static Dress.

On the flip side, she has a soft spot for EDM, especially chillwave and downtempo artists like Odesza, Tycho, and Kasbo.

At the end of the day, nothing beats live music — catch her dead center in the pit, getting her ass kicked and loving every second of it.
